"The Kobe 9 Line"

LATEST KOBE SHOES


Released in 2014, Kobe Bryant’s ninth signature sneaker broke design barriers,

becoming the first basketball silhouette to feature a Flyknit upper.

Stability is optimized through a carbon fiber heel counter and Dynamic Flywire cables.

The Lunarlon midsole is paired with a herringbone outsole based on the pressure-map traction pattern of Bryant’s own foot, reducing slippage.

Discover the Nike Kobe 9, including low-top and high-top iterations and a range of colorways featuring the 'Halo' High and 'Halo' Low and 'Halo' GS.
